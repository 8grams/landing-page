Who We Are

8grams is a small micro development agency, specialized in ReactJS and Flutter

We are a group of Front End Developers, based in Indonesia, living scattered all over the country, and working remotely. We are using Flutter extensively for developing mobile application, and addicted to ReactJS when developing the front end of a web application.

Our Expertise

We are specialized in Front End Development, both for Mobile and Web Application. Flutter is our big gun when dealing with Mobile Application Development, while ReactJS is our ultimate tool when working on Web Development.

Flutter in Mobile Development

Mobile Development is in our heart since the beginning. Even though we are experienced in native development, both Android and iOS, we are fell in love with Flutter. It's simple, straightforward, and makes mobile development fun. It also speeds up the development process because we can create Android and iOS applications at the same time in one single codebase.

ReactJS in Web Development

The demand for Web Development is still in high. That's why we are still willing to help any client who's struggling in web development, especially on the front end side. We have tried many framework and have a conclusion that ReactJS works perfectly on any possible cases. It's beautiful and simple, yet very powerful.

How We Work

On Demand High Skilled Flutter and ReactJS Developers

We don't do projects and we don't manage projects as well. Yet, we deploy one or some of our best Flutter and ReactJS developers to your team. Assign them tasks, invite them to your meetings, fit them with your culture, and consider them as part of your team. Lastly, don't forget to evaluate them and report back to us. If they are underperform, we will give them special treatments, or ultimately, replace them with another developers.
